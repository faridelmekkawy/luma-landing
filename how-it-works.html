How It Works

From RSVP intake to elegant check-in — simple, secure, and fast.

1

Collect Requirements

Use RSVP (Chat) to specify date, venue, estimated guests, and ticket types (e.g., Gold/Silver).

2

Prepare Guest Data

Import Sheets/CSV, apply tiers and +1 rules, and generate unique tokens.

3

Distribute Invites

Send branded digital invitations with secure QR ticket links.

4

On-Site Check-In

Scan at gates via mobile/web scanners; instant validation and counters.

5

Live Analytics

Track arrivals, capacity, and tier performance in real time.

6

Export & Audit

One-click CSV/Sheets export with logs for compliance and follow-up.
