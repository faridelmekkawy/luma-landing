Use Tickets, Store POS, Events, or AI Counseling on their own — or mix them as you need. All our systems run on phones, so you never need special hardware.

Luma Tickets
Invitations, RSVPs, registrations, QR tickets, gate scanning, giveaways and redemptions — all managed from mobile, end to end.
Ideal for ticketed experiences, gated events, and any activation that needs clean guest flows and redemptions.
Luma Store POS
A full POS for retail and F&B that lives on your phone. Track stock, sales, branches, returns, and staff performance — no terminals or extra devices.
Ideal for everyday stores, cafés, kiosks, and pop-ups that want real insights without hardware.
Luma Events
Vendor POS on their phones, event dashboards for owners, stock and sales per brand, and full visibility over what happens during the event.
Ideal for bazaars, markets, brand activations, and experiences where vendors sell and you want to see every inch of the event.
Luma AI Counseling
Sit with a Luma counselor who studies your case and recommends tailored solutions, strategies, and automations for your operation.
Ideal when you know something is not working, but you’re not sure where to start or which systems you actually need.
